Partners

Mort & Co – Australia

Empowering Soil Health and Clean Fuel Innovation

Mort & Co, one of Australia’s leading agribusiness companies, partners with Green Spark Initiative to co-develop sustainable agricultural and biofuel solutions. Together, we’re working to improve soil productivity through joint research, share best practices in regenerative agriculture, and explore scalable opportunities for bio-ethanol production across Africa and Australia.

Areas of Collaboration:

  • SAF production innovation
  • Waste-to-fuel systems
  • Policy engagement on green aviation
“Our shared commitment to regenerative practices and circular economy innovation makes this partnership a powerful force for global impact.”

Mort & Co Global Engagement Team

LanzaTech – USA

Pioneering Sustainable Fuels Through Carbon Recycling

LanzaTech is a global leader in carbon capture and biofuel technology. Through our partnership, Green Spark Initiative is integrating LanzaTech’s cutting-edge microbial fermentation systems to accelerate the production of Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) and clean-burning ethanol across Africa. This alliance puts Africa at the forefront of green energy innovation.

Areas of Collaboration:

  • SAF production innovation
  • Waste-to-fuel systems
  • Policy engagement on green aviation
“Our shared commitment to regenerative practices and circular economy innovation makes this partnership a powerful force for global impact.”
