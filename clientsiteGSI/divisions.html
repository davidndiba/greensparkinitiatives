Farming With Nature

We believe Africa’s farms can nourish both people and planet. Our agriculture division supports smallholder farmers with innovative, affordable, and sustainable tools that restore soil health and improve yields.

Our Programs:

  • Organic Inputs: Bio-fertilizers, improved seeds, and non-toxic pest control tailored to local conditions.
  • Extension Services: Field-based support and agronomic training to help farmers grow with confidence.
  • Input Credit Access: Our flexible input financing models — including produce-as-payment — eliminate up-front cost barriers.
  • Digital Advisory (AI + USSD): Farming advice, pest alerts, weather updates, and market prices via even the simplest phones.
  • Agrovet Franchise Program: Empowering local entrepreneurs to deliver tech-enabled agricultural support in their own communities.

Sustainable Fuels

Clean Energy for a Healthier Future

We’re advancing Africa’s energy future with clean, renewable alternatives that reduce carbon emissions and protect lives.

Our Focus Areas:
  • Bio‐Ethanol Fuel: A cleaner cooking solution that reduces indoor pollution, supports forest conservation, and offers scalable access for households and industry.
  • Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF): We’re pioneering Africa’s entry into low-emission jet fuel — made from renewable biomass and waste sources.
Why It Matters:
  • Reduces dependence on fossil fuels
  • Supports the circular economy
  • Helps decarbonize cooking, transport, and industry

Whether it’s homes, hospitals, hotels, or schools — Terra Plus delivers sparkling clean results without polluting waterways or endangering health.
