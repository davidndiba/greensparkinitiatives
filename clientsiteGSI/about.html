About Us

Growing Africa. Sustainably.

At Green Sparks Initiatives, we believe that doing business in Africa should do more than generate profit — it should regenerate ecosystems, empower communities, and ignite lasting change.

We are a Pan-African impact company headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya, with a bold vision: to build products and services that are good for people and the planet. From restoring soil fertility in rural farms to replacing toxic cleaning products in homes, to fueling Africa’s clean energy future — everything we create is guided by a deep respect for nature and a commitment to justice, innovation, and scale.

Our Mission

To accelerate Africa’s transition to a regenerative, inclusive, and sustainable future through science-backed, people-powered solutions.

What We Do

  • Agriculture – Supporting smallholder farmers with organic inputs, training, credit, and digital advisory tools.
  • Eco-Friendly Cleaning Products – Producing biodegradable, non-toxic cleaning solutions for homes and industries under our Terra Plus brand.
  • Sustainable Fuels – Delivering clean-burning bio-ethanol and pioneering Sustainable Aviation Fuel (SAF) to reduce emissions and deforestation.
