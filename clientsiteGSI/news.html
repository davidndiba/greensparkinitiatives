GSI Signs Landmark MoU with Murang’a County

Date: May 23, 2025

Green Sparks Initiative has signed a Memorandum of Understanding with Murang’a County to scale sustainable farming practices across 10,000 smallholder farms. The partnership includes input financing, AI-powered advisory services, and capacity-building for local agro-dealers. “This is a milestone in community-driven food systems,” said Eric, CEO of GSI.

Farmer Field Visit: Lessons from Eric’s Tour in Siaya

Date: May 23, 2025

Joined farmers in Siaya County to witness the impact of GSI’s bio-fertilizers and drone services firsthand. “Seeing farmers double their yields without harming the land proves our model works,” he said. The visit also included a demonstration of the USSD-based digital advisory platform in local languages.
